The OCS Standard

Our White Glove Turnover Checklist

Every unit follows our meticulous protocol. Nothing overlooked, nothing left to chance.

🥣 Kitchen

  • All cabinets cleaned inside and out
  • Countertops and backsplash scrubbed and sanitized
  • Stove/oven — top, grates, drip pans, interior, hood and filter
  • Refrigerator — interior shelves, drawers, exterior, top
  • Microwave — interior and exterior
  • Dishwasher — interior wipe, edges, and exterior
  • Sink scrubbed, faucet polished, disposal cleaned
  • Floors swept, mopped, and baseboards wiped
  • Light fixtures, switches, and outlets wiped

🛁 Bathrooms

  • Tub/shower scrubbed, grout and tile detailed
  • Shower doors cleaned and de-scaled
  • Toilet — bowl, base, tank, and behind
  • Vanity, sink, and mirror cleaned and polished
  • Cabinets and medicine cabinet wiped inside/out
  • Chrome fixtures polished, hardware wiped
  • Exhaust fan and vent covers cleaned
  • Floors scrubbed, baseboards wiped

🛌 Bedrooms & Living Areas

  • All surfaces dusted — shelves, ledges, mantels
  • Windowsills and window tracks cleaned
  • Blinds dusted and wiped
  • Baseboards wiped throughout
  • Light fixtures, switches, and outlets cleaned
  • Closets — shelves, rods, and floors
  • Doors and door frames wiped
  • Floors swept, mopped, or vacuumed
  • Washer/dryer area cleaned (if applicable)

🛠 Specialized Services

  • Re-cleans and touch-ups for failed inspections
  • Excessive filth remediation (itemized pricing)
  • Rush and same-day turnover service
  • Common area hallways and lobbies
  • Stairwell cleaning
  • Outdoor amenities — grill areas, pool furniture
  • Maintenance office cleaning
  • Recycling program support
